Rebecca's Song

A Love Song 37 Years in the Making

(aka Nancy's Song)

About the Song

Some loves leave an imprint on your heart that time can never erase. This song is a testament to that truth—a melody born from memories of first dances, stolen kisses, and a love that has endured through decades of distance and silence.

Written and performed as a tribute to the girl who became the "It Girl"—the one against whom all others would be measured. Through simple verses and heartfelt chorus, this song captures the bittersweet beauty of a love that never faded, even when life took us in different directions.

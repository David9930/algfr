About A Letter Guide For Rebecca

A captivating science fiction novel that explores the encounter between humanity and an alien intelligence. When Rebecca receives a mysterious letter, she's drawn into an adventure beyond her wildest dreams.

This thought-provoking story combines elements of mystery, adventure, and philosophical questions about our place in the universe.
